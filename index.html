My Expertises

Tableau Desktop

Dive into my Tableau dashboards to see how I turn complex datasets into visually appealing and interactive stories. My projects range from consumer behavior analyses to financial performance reviews, all designed to enhance decision-making processes with clear, actionable insights

Microsoft Office Suite

Experience how I harness the full potential of Microsoft Office tools to optimize data processes. My expertise includes developing complex Excel formulas, macros, and pivot tables, integrating seamlessly with tools like Access and Outlook to streamline operations and enhance productivity.

Power Automate

Discover my solutions in automating routine data tasks using Power Automate. I create custom flows that connect services, process data, and send notifications, all tailored to reduce manual workloads and increase efficiency..

SQL

Explore the backbone of my data manipulation skills through SQL. I build, query, and manage databases with precision, utilizing SQL to perform data integration, migration, and complex joins that underpin analytical projects and reports.

Python
